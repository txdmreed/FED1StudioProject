Who Are You?

Robotics 101

i

Begginner - Learn the basics of robotic technical jargon, code and structure. Master the vocabulary of the craft.

Intermediate - Actually begin crafting your new robot minion.

Advanced - Master the rules of robotics and how to get around them.

How to Manage a Work/Life Seperation for Dummies

Beginner - Happy hour basics. When to go and when to invite your coworkers, including how to behave around your boss.

Intermediate - How to have good clean fun in the work place, and how to pull off inappropriate work place behavior without being caught.

Advanced - How to properly play hooky and get the most out of it.

How to Identify Your Nerd People

Beginner - Learning the tells of the average nerd and how to recognize them in the wild.

Intermediate - A deeper dive into the characteristics of specific geekcomunities, as well as potential territories where these species may be found.

Advanced - Learn more complex skills of blending in and ingratiating yourself into the local nerd population.


There Is No Chair: How to Unplug and Embrace Seated the Truth

Beginner - Wall sitting, and other activities to build core and leg strength.

Intermediate - The recliner is a lie. Learning to spot the fake news and unrealities of the lounging life.

Advanced - The standing desk, learning to embrace the hard realities of the non-sitting world.

All About Blinkers and How to Use Them

Whatever happens here will create its own timeline, its own reality, a temporal tipping point. The future revolves around you, here, now, so do good!

Robotics 202: How to Make Your Robots Less Creepy and More Relatable

There are fixed points throughout time where things must stay exactly the way they are. This is not one of them. This is an opportunity!
